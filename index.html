Accommodation

Where to stay

We will be having the Wedding at Rudding Park where there are a variety of Hotel Rooms. If you want to look elsewhere there are lots of hotels in central Harrogate, most of these will be within a 15 minute drive of the wedding venue. If you want to spend a bit longer in the area we recommend hiring a cottage. If you have a specific location/hotel in mind send us the details and we can check location. There is a list of links below to get you started but you should look at the usual booking sites such as booking.com /air bnb etc.

  • Rudding Park Hotel
  • Yorkshire Hotel
  • Travel Lodge
  • The InnKeeper's Lodge

Things to do

If you have not been to Harrogate before there is loads to do. Check out some of the links below. Let us know if you are staying a bit longer and we can help you with recommendations.

  • Bettys - Great for classic afternoon tea
  • Valley Gardens - great for a relaxed stroll
  • Brimham Rocks - Great for a bit of adventure (for outdoor climbing also check out Almscliffe Cragg, Ilkely Moor)
  • Fountains Abbey
  • Harlow Carr - Great for a relaxed afternoon walk and cakes at Bettys!
  • Knaresborough - Great for a ride on the boats, great views and cute shops.
  • York - Awesomely Harry Potter -esque city, you have to visit the Shambles!
